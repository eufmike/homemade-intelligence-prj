# Methodology & Vision

> The analytical philosophy behind Homemade Intelligence.

---

## Vision

Build a **personal, unbiased intelligence platform** that synthesizes geopolitical and financial information from diverse global sources — producing actionable analysis reports tailored to multiple audiences, with emphasis on **US-interested topics** and **East Asian dynamics including Taiwan strait safety**.

This is not a news aggregator. It is a structured analytical system designed to:

- **Triangulate** across ideologically and geographically distinct sources
- **Detect bias** rather than pretend it doesn't exist
- **Generate reports** in formats appropriate for different audiences and literacy levels
- **Bridge the information gap** between English-language and Mandarin-language geopolitical discourse

---

## Problem Statement

1. **Mainstream media consolidation** creates blind spots — no single outlet covers the full picture of US politics, East Asian security, and global financial markets without editorial bias.
2. **Language barriers** prevent Mandarin-speaking audiences (especially in Taiwan) from accessing high-quality, triangulated geopolitical analysis typically available only in English.
3. **Generational accessibility gaps** mean that critical information about Taiwan strait safety never reaches the people most affected — including Taiwanese elders (65+) who rely on media ecosystems vulnerable to manipulation.
4. **Investment decisions** are routinely made without adequate consideration of geopolitical risk — particularly energy chokepoint exposure, compound vulnerability windows, and non-Western perspectives on market-moving events.

---

## Core Analytical Principles

| Principle | Description |
|---|---|
| **Triangulation over trust** | No single source is authoritative. Cross-reference across ideologically and geographically distinct outlets. |
| **Bias as a variable** | Every source has a perspective. The platform tracks and labels bias — it doesn't pretend objectivity exists. |
| **Leading over lagging** | Prioritize leading indicators (VIX, CDS, PMI, tanker flows) over lagging confirmations (GDP, official statements). |
| **Non-Western perspectives required** | Every analysis must include at least one non-Anglophone source to avoid epistemic capture. |
| **Audience-first design** | Reports are shaped by who reads them — an elder in Taipei and an analyst in Washington need different outputs from the same intelligence. |
| **Manipulation awareness** | Every report considers whether the underlying information may be subject to coordinated inauthentic behavior (CIB). |
| **Transparency of method** | Reports include confidence levels, source lists, and methodology notes. |

## Intelligence Source Architecture (v2.0)

The platform uses a 10-layer source architecture. Version 2.0 was updated following the analytical stress-test during the February 28, 2026 US-Israel joint strike on Iran (Operation Epic Fury), which exposed gaps in energy intelligence, Persian-language sources, and compound vulnerability analysis.

See [`references/knowledgebase/international-situation-intelligence-stack.md`](../references/knowledgebase/international-situation-intelligence-stack.md) for the full source list with URLs and analytical notes.

| # | Layer | Analytical Role |
|---|---|---|
| I | Geopolitics & Strategic Affairs | Structural forces, think tanks, journals |
| II | Political News — Western Anglophone | Primary record sources; bias tracked across spectrum |
| III | Economics & Policy | Macroeconomic trends, trade, fiscal/monetary policy |
| IV | Energy, Commodities & Chokepoints *(v2.0)* | Energy markets as geopolitical transmission mechanism |
| V | Financial Markets | Leading indicators, commodity derivatives |
| VI | Non-English & Non-Western Sources | Avoids Anglophone epistemic capture |
| VII | Early Warning Indexes & Quantitative Dashboards | VIX, CDS, ACLED, BDI, Compound Vulnerability scoring |
| VIII | Trump Behavior Prediction Stack | 7 empirical behavioral heuristics + prediction markets |
| IX | Social Media & AI Manipulation Analysis | CIB detection, synthetic media, narrative laundering |
| X | Integrated Monitoring Workflow | Temporal layer structure, source priority matrix |

### Key Analytical Frameworks

- **Compound Vulnerability Framework** — Scores target states across military, economic, political, diplomatic, and information dimensions to identify windows of maximum exposure.
- **Trump Behavior Prediction (7 heuristics)** — Including *market crash → policy reversal* (primary constraint) and *negotiation as cover for military planning* (added v2.0).
- **Real-Time Manipulation Detection (9-signal CIB checklist)** — Applied to all time-sensitive analysis before publication.
- **Chokepoint Monitoring** — 5 critical maritime chokepoints: Hormuz, Suez, Malacca, Bosphorus, Taiwan Strait.
- **Analytical Failure Mode Reference (7 modes)** — Anglophone epistemic capture, negotiation optimism bias, nuclear pretext conflation, monocausal attribution, recency bias, confirmation bias, energy market blindspot.

## Target Audiences & Output Standards

### English — Full Analytical
- Professional tone comparable to think tank publications
- Full citations, confidence levels (High / Medium / Low) per finding
- Dissenting views section, manipulation check, source list with bias labels
- Sections: Executive Summary → Key Findings → Source Divergence → Analysis → Alternatives → Manipulation Check → Implications → Confidence → Sources

### Traditional Chinese — General Taiwanese (繁體中文)
- Taiwan-standard terminology only (總統 not 領導人; 中共 for CCP)
- Note narrative divergences explicitly for Taiwan-relevant topics
- Cross-strait risk implications highlighted

### Traditional Chinese — Elder-Accessible (長輩版)
- Short sentences (≤20 characters where possible)
- Parenthetical jargon explanations: 通貨膨脹（物價上漲）
- Traffic light risk indicators: 🟢 安全 / 🟡 注意 / 🔴 警戒
- 謠言警示 section addressing active LINE/Facebook misinformation
- Warm, respectful tone; designed to be read aloud or shared on LINE
